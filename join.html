Become a Member

Why become a member? Membership in SOFREE gives you preferred status and discounts for events, a monthly bulletin, and advance notice about SOFREE services. Membership fees are kept at a minimum so that you can support your favourite SOFREE activities with income tax deductible donations (the membership fees themselves are not tax deductible).

Special membership fees are available for seniors, households and students; membership for students who belong to a student atheist group is free. Students: Please email our secretary to join! Include your email address, your student group, your school, and student number. Students under the age of eighteen will require the signed permission of a parent or guardian.

To join SoFree, please sign up using the following PayPal link after selecting the type of membership you would like to purchase. Please provide us with your email address and telephone number when making your payment through PayPal. We will use your email address to send you our bulletin as well as conveying other important matters to you. Your information will be kept private: it will not be shared with third parties or used for any non-SoFree related activity.
